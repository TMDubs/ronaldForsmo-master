Books

I opened the eagerly awaited package carefully as instructed – I read EVERYTHING from the beginning – then of course ran to the bathroom as I was laughing out loud and – well, you know, old ladies who have had several babies have VERY weak bladders – Still laughing as I type, adored the VERY LONG RUN-ON SENTENCE called Autumn...your brain is undoubtedly willed to science! My dear boy – wohin next? Climbing the Alps with a wineskin full of an amusing red wine? Sewing a shadow on the heels of a heel? Poling a gondola through the Venetian canals with a robust young tenor shouting O Solo Mio off key? I shall cook an absolutely fabulously weird meal this night to celebrate. Incidentally, given your incredible sense of humor and irony, you are infinitely more interesting than Henry James.

— Anne G., Rockport, MA

Everyone should have a copy of this book in their library. Your writing talent is unequaled. A true gem!

— Monika Renee Cosson, Gulf Breeze, FL

How you have chosen to organize this compendium works beautifully: They seem as random and scattered (and de-ranged v. arranged. YAY!) as the pieces themselves! I'll say first that I don't see how anyone can NOT enjoy your writing. If you don't like "Refracted Fairy Tales," hop on over to "...Confabulations" or "...Limericks" That is high praise indeed, that there is something for everyone happily contained herein. If I had to choose this instant, I like best – so far – oh dear, this is harder than I thought! I read right straight through "Bios Degradable" which gives dead away your extensive experience in performing arts. I do not let anything keep me from laughing out loud (like the looks of perfect strangers, who chose to remain that way, some making a wide berth away from the cackling lady in the beat-up beach chair).

— Carol Furtwangler, Charleston, SC

Your brilliance (oh!) I've long known – so glad your essays, poems & observations to a book have grown.

Both myself and my ever ready "ulnar draped humerus" look eagerly forward to future additions and editions!
